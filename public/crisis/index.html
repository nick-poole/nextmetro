If You're in Crisis

If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or emotional distress, help is available right now.

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline Call or text 988 988lifeline.org
  • Crisis Text Line Text HOME to 741741 crisistextline.org
  • SAMHSA National Helpline Call 1-800-662-4357 samhsa.gov
  • Veterans Crisis Line Call 988, then press 1 veteranscrisisline.net

You are not alone. These services are free, confidential, and available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
