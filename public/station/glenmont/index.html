    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro line serves Glenmont station?

    Glenmont is the eastern terminus of the Red Line. The other terminus is Shady Grove.

    Does Glenmont station have parking?

    Yes, Glenmont station has a large WMATA-operated parking garage.

    What are the hours of operation for Glenmont station?

    Glenmont station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.

    Is Glenmont the last stop on the Red Line?

    Yes, Glenmont is the eastern terminus of the Red Line. All Red Line trains traveling toward Glenmont terminate here.
