    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro line serves McLean station?

    McLean Metro station is served by the Silver Line. The Silver Line runs between Ashburn and Downtown Largo.

    Is there parking at McLean Metro station?

    Yes, McLean Metro station offers a parking garage with over 5,000 spaces available for daily and reserved parking.

    Is McLean Metro station accessible?

    Yes, McLean Metro station is fully accessible with elevators and escalators. The station is ADA compliant.

    What are the hours of operation for McLean Metro station?

    McLean Metro station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.
