    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro line serves Dupont Circle?

    Dupont Circle is served by the Red Line, between Woodley Park-Zoo/Adams Morgan and Farragut North.

    Does Dupont Circle have parking?

    No, Dupont Circle does not have WMATA parking. Street parking and private garages are available in the area.

    What are the hours of operation for Dupont Circle station?

    Dupont Circle station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.

    What is near Dupont Circle station?

    Nearby attractions include Dupont Circle park and fountain, Embassy Row, Phillips Collection art gallery, Connecticut Avenue shops, and numerous restaurants and bars.
