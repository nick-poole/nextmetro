    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro lines serve Farragut North station?

    Farragut North is served by the Red Line, running between Shady Grove in Maryland and Glenmont in Maryland.

    Is Farragut North station accessible?

    Yes, Farragut North station is fully accessible with elevators and escalators connecting the street level to the platform. The station is ADA compliant.

    What is the difference between Farragut North and Farragut West?

    Farragut North serves the Red Line, while Farragut West serves the Orange, Blue, and Silver lines. The two stations are about a block apart and are not connected by an underground transfer. You must exit one station and walk to the other.

    What are the hours of operation for Farragut North station?

    Farragut North station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.
