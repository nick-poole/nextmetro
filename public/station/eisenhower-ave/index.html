    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro lines serve Eisenhower Avenue station?

    Eisenhower Avenue is served exclusively by the Yellow Line, running between Fort Totten and Huntington.

    What is near Eisenhower Avenue station?

    Eisenhower Avenue station serves the Eisenhower Valley commercial corridor in Alexandria, including the Hoffman Town Center, the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office, and numerous office buildings and hotels.

    Does Eisenhower Avenue station have parking?

    Yes, Eisenhower Avenue has a WMATA-operated parking garage with approximately 1,000 spaces available for Metro riders.

    What are the hours of operation for Eisenhower Avenue station?

    Eisenhower Avenue station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.
