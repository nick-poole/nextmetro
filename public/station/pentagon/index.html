    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro lines serve Pentagon station?

    Pentagon Metro station is served by both the Blue Line and the Yellow Line. The Blue Line runs between Franconia-Springfield and Downtown Largo, while the Yellow Line runs between Huntington and Greenbelt.

    Is Pentagon Metro station accessible to the public?

    Yes, the Metro station is open to the public. However, access to the Pentagon building itself requires proper credentials. The station connects to the Pentagon Transit Center for bus connections.

    Is Pentagon Metro station accessible?

    Yes, Pentagon Metro station is fully accessible with elevators and escalators connecting the mezzanine level to the platform. The station is ADA compliant.

    What are the hours of operation for Pentagon Metro station?

    Pentagon Metro station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.
