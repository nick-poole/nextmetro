    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro line serves Southern Ave station?

    Southern Ave is served by the Green Line, located between Naylor Road and Congress Heights stations.

    What are the hours of operation for Southern Ave station?

    Southern Ave station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.

    Is parking available at Southern Ave station?

    No, Southern Ave station does not offer parking facilities. Riders are encouraged to use bus connections or drop-off options.

    What is near Southern Ave Metro station?

    Southern Ave station is located at the District of Columbia and Maryland border, serving the Oxon Hill community and Southeast DC neighborhoods.
