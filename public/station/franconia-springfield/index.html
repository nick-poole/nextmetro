    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro lines serve Franconia-Springfield station?

    Franconia-Springfield is the western terminus of the Blue Line. All Blue Line trains originate or terminate here, traveling east toward Downtown Largo via King St-Old Town and Downtown DC.

    What is near Franconia-Springfield Metro station?

    Franconia-Springfield station is a major transit hub located near the Springfield Town Center, the Springfield interchange of I-95/I-395/I-495, and the Fort Belvoir area. It serves as a Park & Ride facility for Northern Virginia commuters.

    Is parking available at Franconia-Springfield station?

    Yes, Franconia-Springfield has one of the largest parking facilities in the Metro system with approximately 5,000 spaces across multiple garages and surface lots.

    What are the hours of operation for Franconia-Springfield station?

    Franconia-Springfield station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.
