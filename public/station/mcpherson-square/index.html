    Frequently Asked Questions

    What Metro lines serve McPherson Square station?

    McPherson Square station is served by the Orange, Blue, and Silver Lines.

    What is near McPherson Square station?

    McPherson Square station is near McPherson Square park, the White House (two blocks south), and the K Street business corridor. Numerous office buildings, restaurants, and hotels are within walking distance.

    Does McPherson Square station have parking?

    McPherson Square station does not have a WMATA-operated parking facility. Limited metered street parking and private garages are available in the surrounding downtown area.

    What are the hours of operation for McPherson Square station?

    McPherson Square station is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 AM to midnight, Friday from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM, and Sunday from 7:00 AM to midnight.
