    The Short Version

    NextMetro is a transit information app. We collect minimal data — basically just standard server logs and whatever analytics we use to understand traffic. We don't sell your data. We're not affiliated with WMATA. If you're in the EU, California, or other places with strong privacy laws, you have rights we respect.

    We use Google Fonts, which means Google gets your IP address when you load our site. We're working on self-hosting fonts to eliminate this.

    By using NextMetro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

    5. Third-Party Services

    The Service uses the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

    5.1 Google Fonts

    What it is: NextMetro uses Google Fonts to display typography.

    What Google collects: When you load the Service, your browser downloads font files from Google's servers. Google receives:

    • Your IP address
    • The URL of the page you're visiting (referrer)
    • Browser and device information
    • Date and time of access

    Google's use: Google states they use this data to serve fonts efficiently and compile aggregate usage statistics. See Google's Privacy Policy and Google Fonts FAQ.

    Your options:

    • Use a browser extension to block Google Fonts
    • Use a privacy-focused browser that blocks third-party requests

    Our commitment: We are working toward self-hosting fonts to eliminate this third-party data transfer.

    5.2 Hosting Provider

    What it is: NextMetro is hosted by Cloudflare.

    What they collect: Standard server logs including IP addresses, request data, and timestamps.

    Their use: Providing hosting services, security, and abuse prevention.

    More information: See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

    5.3 Analytics

    We currently do not use third-party analytics services. We rely only on standard server logs.

    5.4 WMATA API

    What it is: Transit data displayed in NextMetro is fetched from WMATA's public API.

    Data flow: NextMetro's servers request data from WMATA's servers. Your personal information is NOT transmitted to WMATA through your use of NextMetro. WMATA does not receive your IP address or any identifying information from NextMetro.
